# Installation
## Manual
1. Ensure you have BepInEx installed. [see installation guide](https://docs.bepinex.dev/articles/user_guide/installation/index.html)
2. Download [Configgy](https://thunderstore.io/c/ultrakill/p/Hydraxous/Configgy/), [FixPluginTypesSerialization](https://thunderstore.io/c/ultrakill/p/averyocean65/FixPluginTypesSerialization/) and [AUU](https://thunderstore.io/c/ultrakill/p/averyocean65/AUU/) and extract them into either your `BepInEx/plugins` folder, or `BepInEx/patchers` for `FixPluginTypesSerialization`.
3. Export the `UltraPortal` folder from the zip file into the `BepInEx/plugins` folder.
4. Enjoy!

## r2modman / Thunderstore Mod Manager
1. Download [r2modman](https://r2modman.com/download-latest/) (if you haven't already.)
2. Upon launching r2modman, select ULTRAKILL as the game you'd like to mod. (skip if ULTRAKILL is already your default. If you have another game set as your default, click on the underlined `Back to game selection` near the top of the screen!)
3. Open up or create a profile.
4. Go to the `Online` tab on the left side of the screen.
5. Scroll through the mod list or search for `UltraPortal` in the search bar. (make sure to download `UltraPortal` by **averyocean65**, not the utility library by `Temporary`!)
6. Once you've pressed on `UltraPortal`, click the `Download` button on the right side of the screen.
7. Select your preferred version and press `Download with dependencies`.
8. After everything has finished downloading, click on the `Start modded` button at the top of the screen.
9. Enjoy!

# How To Use?
Once in a level, you will be able to press `7` (can be changed in settings) to pull up the Portal Gun or the Mirror Gun. Beware that equipping either gun within a level causes your score/leaderboard submission for that level to be DISABLED, once you finish the level you will see a text saying `- USED PORTAL GUN` to indicate the invalid run.

## The Portal Gun
The Portal Gun is the blue variation, it allows you to shoot two portals using your primary or alternative fire.
![screenshot_0.jpg](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/averyocean65/UltraPortal/master/Thunderstore/media/screenshot_0.jpg)

## The Mirror Gun
The Mirror Gun is the green variation, it allows you to shoot one big mirror portal using your primary fire. With the alternative fire you can shoot a mirror that flips the world upside down, how whimsical!
![screenshot_1.jpg](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/averyocean65/UltraPortal/master/Thunderstore/media/screenshot_1.jpg)

## The Twist Gun
The Twist Gun is the red variation, it lets you shoot two portals that, upon entering them, rotate the world based on what way they face. It's confusing at first, but once you figure it out, it can be very fun to mess around with.
![screenshot_4.jpg](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/averyocean65/UltraPortal/master/Thunderstore/media/screenshot_4.jpg)
